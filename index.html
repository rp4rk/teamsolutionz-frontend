Problem: Non breaking backwards compatibility.

Solution: Extension of existing tech.

UX Concerns:
  • Had to be accessible and discoverable.
  • Had to accept overrides.
  • Had to be an improvement over the existing solution.

Problem: Large amount of parameters.

Solution: Filters and dynamic lists.

UX Concerns:
  • Some fields had hundreds of potential parameters.
  • Usability on all platforms, especially iPad.
  • Had to be able to function on the iPad application (eventually) with some consistency.

Problem: Constructing the functions menu, without the correct data returned.

Solution: Communicating with the third party to attain top secret parameters for their API.

UX Concerns:
  • Switching from the third party tool to our own could cause confusion.
  • BCIS are highly standardized, missing functions could result in legal action.

Problem: Allowing a selection of price from hundreds of price points.

Solution: Categorically presenting the information in a 'Z layout' format.

UX Concerns:
  • Certain functions may not be populated.
  • Allowing the selection of the 'sample' field.
